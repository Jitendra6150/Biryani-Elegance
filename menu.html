Biryani Eligence About Our MENU

Welcome to Biryani Elegance Restaurant, where every dish tells a story of tradition and flavor. Our menu features a delightful selection of Indian cuisine, carefully crafted to tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the vibrant streets of India. From fragrant biryanis to succulent kebabs, each dish is prepared with the finest ingredients and authentic spices, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience. Explore our menu below and embark on a culinary journey through the diverse flavors of India.

Indulge in our appetizers, where crispy wings, flavorful chicken tikka, and crunchy vegetable pakoras await to kickstart your meal with a burst of flavor. For those seeking a lighter option, our soups and salads offer a refreshing start, featuring the rich and comforting Mulligatawny Soup and the crisp freshness of our Mixed Green Salad.

For our vegetarian patrons, we offer a tempting array of entrees, including the aromatic Vegetable Biryani, the creamy Paneer Tikka Masala, and the comforting Dal Makhani. Meanwhile, our non-vegetarian selection boasts mouthwatering delights such as the fragrant Chicken Biryani, the rich and robust Lamb Rogan Josh, and the zesty Fish Curry.

Save room for dessert as our sweet treats are sure to delight your senses. Indulge in the decadent sweetness of Gulab Jamun, savor the creamy richness of Kheer, or cool off with the refreshing Mango Lassi.

Pair your meal with one of our delightful beverages, including the aromatic Masala Chai, the creamy Lassi available in sweet or salted variations, or choose from our selection of soft drinks and juices.
